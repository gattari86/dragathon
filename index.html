Visual Style

  • Overall Feel: Clean, modern aesthetic with a soft, nurturing touch.
  • Space: Generous use of white space (Soft Cream background) for a light, uncluttered, and breathable feel.
  • Elements: Minimal, softly rounded icons. Avoid heavy textures or complex gradients.
  • Photography: Authentic, real-life photography featuring natural light (especially for Before & Afters).

Crafting the Experience: Layout & Typography

A well-defined structure and clear typographic hierarchy ensure visitors can navigate easily and absorb information effectively.

Typography Size Recommendations

  • Hero Headline: 48–60px, Semi Bold
  • Subhead/Tagline: 20–24px, Regular
  • Section Headings: 32–36px, Semi Bold
  • Body Text: 16–18px, Regular
  • CTA Buttons: 16px, Semi Bold (Example: Button Text)

Layout & Spacing

  • Grid System: Flexible 12-column grid (32px gutters).
  • Content Width: Max width 1140–1200px.
  • Section Spacing: 80–100px top/bottom padding.
  • Page Padding: 32px (mobile), 64px (desktop) side padding.

Design Direction Overview: Ensuring Consistency & Clarity

This summarizes the core visual and structural principles guiding the website's design and development.

  • Layout System: 12-column grid, max-width (1140-1200px), section padding (80-100px), page padding (32px mobile, 64px desktop).
  • Typography: Consistent Poppins (Headers) & Open Sans (Body/CTAs) usage per size guidelines. Focus on readability.
  • Color Palette: Strategic use of Soft Cream (bg), Deep Teal (text), Mint Green/Light Grey (accents), Bright Aqua (highlights/CTAs). High contrast for accessibility.
  • Calls-to-Action (CTAs): Uniform style (Open Sans Semi Bold, 16px), clear text, prominent placement.
  • Photography: High-quality, authentic, natural light photos. Consistent B&A shots.
  • Visual Elements: Minimal, rounded icons. Subtle dividers/whitespace. Light backgrounds.
  • Accessibility: Strong contrast, readable fonts, logical structure.
